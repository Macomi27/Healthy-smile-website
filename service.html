Services





Restorative Dentistry


When tooth decay appears, we act quickly and effectively. Restorative dentistry involves removing cavities and rebuilding teeth using modern composite materials. We focus on both the aesthetics and durability of fillings, ensuring results that are not only functional but also virtually invisible.






Endodontics (root canal treatment)


In cases of advanced pulp inflammation, we perform root canal treatment using a surgical microscope and digital radiography. This allows us to carry out procedures with maximum precision, safety, and predictability. Our goal is to preserve the patient’s natural teeth for as long as possible.





Orthodontics


When tooth decay appears, we act quickly and effectively. Orthodontic treatment involves correcting the alignment of teeth and bite using modern methods tailored to each patient. Our goal is not only a healthy, functional smile, but also one that looks great and builds confidence.






Cosmetic dentistry


Want to enhance the appearance of your smile? We offer a range of cosmetic treatments, including professional teeth whitening (in-office, laser, and tray-based), composite and porcelain veneers, and bonding. These procedures allow us to improve the color, shape, and alignment of your teeth quickly and safely.

Prosthetic dentistry


Missing teeth are not only an aesthetic issue but also a functional one. We offer modern prosthetic solutions — crowns, bridges, removable and fixed dentures, as well as implant-supported restorations. Our goal is to restore full chewing function and the natural appearance of your smile.






Preventive care


Prevention is the foundation of a healthy smile. Regular check-ups and professional hygiene treatments help prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues. We offer scaling, air polishing, fluoride treatments, and personalized hygiene instructions — all to keep your teeth healthy for many years to come.
